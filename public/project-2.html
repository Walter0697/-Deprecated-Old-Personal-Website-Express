Ball Game

A coding practice game that based on the game rule of a mobile app game : BBTAN from 111%


Basic game rules of BBTAN:

Player will have ball(s) that can shoot at where they point to, balls will damage the boxes and deduct 1 hp/number from them, and bounce around between balls and walls.

The number of balls will be increased after one of the balls hit the circle with "+" sign.

Balls will stop after they hit the ground. After all balls stop, all balls will be repositioned to a same location where the first ball stopped. Also, all boxes will go down by one block and generate a new row of boxes at the top of the screen. Hp/number of the boxes will increase per round.

If one box hit the ground, the game will be ended. Therefore, player should destroy the boxes as quick as they can by aimming the suitable position.


Design:

Purchase system to unlock skins or abilities

Abilities that different from the original game:

->tool that can spawn small balls to all direction and damage the boxes

->tool that can show the invisible boxes (Boxes will turn invisible after a certain scores in my game)

->tool that will randomly hit boxes

->tool that will make the balls stop repositioning in the next round

->tool that will add a prediction line in the next round

->bomb that will damage the boxes within 3x3 size around it

->X-ray that will generate two diagonal line to damage any boxes that collide with
