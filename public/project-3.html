Imitator

A game that allows player to imitate the enemies' bullets


Design:

Bullet type

Default bullet : a bullet that will shoot forward

Two-way bullet : two bullets that will shoot in two sides

Long bullet : bullet that is long and have bigger damage

Shot gun : bullet that have a short range but with a large amounts of damage

Fire ball : bullet that will have a chance to make enemies on fire

Grenade Launcher : will shoot a grenade that will explode after collision

Random Shot : will shot at random direction

Light Ball : can go though enemies and provide continuous damage

Homing bullet : will trace enemies within a certain range

Lazer : a straight line that provide damage to all enemies that touch it

Blast shot : a certain number of bullets that shoot to the front with different angel

Firework : shooting a small bullet that will shoot split into tons of bullets in all direction

Rocket : provide damage and explosion after collision

Lazer Beam : a thicc lazer that will provide huge damage to the enemies that touch it


Tools:

Green Shield : Shield that will increase the size when multiple green shields are picked up

Yellow Shield : Shield that have health, the bigger health it has, the larger it will be

Blue Shield : Shield that can replace health, will slowly decrease when not used

Health Pack : will recover health

Imumium : Allow player to hold more bullet types at once

Auspicious Stone : Increase the chance for enemies to drop tools
