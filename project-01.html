Music Shooter

This is normal horizontal scrolling game for learning the concept of vertical scrooling.


Basic control:

Use W,A,S,D to move

Use SpaceBar to shoot


Design:

Full screen health bar which will cover the screen when low health

Random enemies generate (can switch to unlimited mode with little edits)

Most assets were drawn by myself such as player, enemies and bullets, and the others are from open game art
